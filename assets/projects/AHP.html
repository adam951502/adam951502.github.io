Comparison of sustainability assessment methods for electricity generation technologies

  • Tech Stack: Multi-criteria decision making, TOPSIS, WSM, AHP, python

Assessing sustainability of electricity generation technologies using multi-criteria decision methods like WSM(Weighted Sum Model), TOPSIS(Technique for Order Preference by Similarity to Ideal Solution), and AHP(Analytic Hierarchy Process). These methods help select the most suitable technology based on specific criteria with different weights, resulting in scores and rankings. Many types of decision methods are available, each with unique characteristics and approaches for ranking technologies.
