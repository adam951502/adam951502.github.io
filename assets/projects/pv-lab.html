Processing and analysis of raw spectroscopic data from microalbumin urine test

  • Tech Stack: monocrystalline solar sell, multicrystalline solar cell, bypass diode, IV-curves, python, LaTeX

As there exists many different applications as well as due to the demand of higher efficiency, various solar cell types have been developed. Significantly, the crystalline silicon solar cells with the most popular and utilized variations of them being the monocrystalline and multicrystalline solar cell. An experiment was made to study the characteristics of such cells, as well as study their characteristics by mimicking real life conditions by studying the effect of a bypass diode on a module of the aforementioned solar cell types. Measurements were made, results were recorded and IV curves at different scenarios were plotted to show such effects.
