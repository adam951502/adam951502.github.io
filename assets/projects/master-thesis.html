Modeling of the failure of a single-lap hybrid joint under tensile loading

  • Tech Stack: FEM, Ls-dyna, HyperMesh, Linux, shell script, composite, cohesive zone model, python

The project aimed to simulate different hybrid assemblies made of aluminum and carbon fiber reinforced polymer (CFRP). The simulation focused on the behavior of the hybrid adhesive bonded joints, hybrid mechanical fasten joints, and hybrid bonded-fasten joints using finite element method modeling.
