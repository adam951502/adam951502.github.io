Modeling the low velocity impact failure (delamination) of different CFRP configurations under various scenarios in LS-DYNA

  • Tech Stack: FEM, Ls-dyna, HyperMesh, Linux, shell script, composite, cohesive zone model, ASTM D7136, ball drop test, python

The project utilized LS-DYNA software and finite element method (FEM) to simulate the delamination failure in CFRP under low-velocity impact test conditions as per ASTM D7136 standards. Different configurations of CFRP and experimental scenarios were considered for the simulations.
